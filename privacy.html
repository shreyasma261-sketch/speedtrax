SpeedTrax Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

SpeedTrax is a client-side Progressive Web App. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

The app collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • GPS location data (used only for speed calculation, not stored persistently)
  • Trip history (distance, speed, duration)
  • Fuel log entries
  • Vehicle information you enter
  • Expense records

2. Location Data

SpeedTrax requests access to your device's GPS/location to calculate your real-time speed. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to external servers.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. Third-Party Services

SpeedTrax does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through Settings → Clear All Data, or by clearing your browser's site data for this app.
